Save To Spotify

To connect your Spotify account, you need a one-time setup in the Spotify Developer Dashboard.

  1. Sign in, click “Create app”, and give it any name.
  2. Open the app → Settings → “Redirect URIs” → click “Add Redirect URI”.
  3. Paste the URI shown below, then click “Save”.
  4. Copy the “Client ID” from the app’s overview and paste it here.
